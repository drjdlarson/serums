Cauchy

class Cauchy(location=None, scale=None)[source]

Bases: StudentsT

Represents a Cauchy distribution.

This is a special case of the Student’s t-distribution with the degrees of freedom fixed at 1. However, the mean and covariance do not exist for this distribution.
